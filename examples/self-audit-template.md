## 1. Past Will `P`

Past will is evidence.

```txt
What has already happened?
What choices, habits, artifacts, promises, skills, scars, failures, or repairs exist?
What patterns have repeated?
What does the past prove?
What does the past merely suggest?
What past evidence is being over-weighted?
What past evidence is being ignored?
```

## 2. Present Will `N`

Present will is verification.

```txt
What is being chosen now?
What is being refused now?
What is attention currently attached to?
What is being clarified before interpretation?
What claim is being verified now?
What action would verify reality without overcommitting?
```

## 3. Future Will `F`

Future will is attractor.

```txt
What future is being selected for?
What capability is being protected or developed?
What future self, state, system, or world is being made imaginable?
What future would make present sacrifice meaningful?
What present evidence shows this path is real?
What future story may be drifting into fantasy or coercive vision?
```


## 4. Invariant Will `I`

Invariant will is conserved structure.

```txt
What remains true across change?
What principle, commitment, or agency-pattern survives state shifts?
What is not merely mood, brand, aesthetic, social reception, approval, or temporary desire?
What lets the past predict without dominating?
What lets the future guide without deluding?
What is too rigid?
What is too vague?
What might be externally assigned misrepresentation?
```

```txt

```

## 5. Variable Will `V`

Variable will is adaptation.

```txt
What changed?
Why did it change?
Was the change internal, external, social, biological, strategic, informational, or coercive?
Is this self-adaptation, social-adaptation, or invariant-breaking?
What state is silently steering agency?
What context is shaping behavior without being named?
```

## 6. Relational Will `R`

Relational will is coherence topology.

```txt
How do the components affect each other?
Which component is dominating?
Which component is being ignored?
Where is coherence forming?
Where is distortion entering?
Where is repair needed?
How should the components relate so agency becomes stronger without flattening distinction?
```
